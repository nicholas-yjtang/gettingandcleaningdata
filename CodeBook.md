# Data Dictionary #

subject Integer
	The subject ID of the collected data.
		1..30 .Unique identifier assigned for each subject  

activity Factor
	The type of activity performed when the measurement was performed
		1. WALKING
		2. WALKING_UPSTAIRS
		3. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
		4. SITTING
		5. STANDING
		6. LAYING

subject Numeric  
 	The mean of the collected subject per subject/activity pair  
 		-1 to 1 .The normalized value mean for each subject/activity pair  
  
activity Numeric  
 	The mean of the collected activity per subject/activity pair  
 		-1 to 1 .The normalized value mean for each subject/activity pair
